### Week 1 Objectives:

* Connect and get acquainted with members and mentors of First Cloud Journey / AWS internship group.
* Understand the internship rules, working method, and weekly reporting requirements.
* Learn basic AWS service groups and become familiar with AWS Management Console and AWS CLI.
* Study fundamental AWS services such as EC2, S3, and IAM at an introductory level.
* Learn how AWS workshops are structured and complete the basic AWS Console Home tasks to receive promotional credits.
* Watch and learn from the Module 01 playlist to get an initial overview of cloud concepts.
