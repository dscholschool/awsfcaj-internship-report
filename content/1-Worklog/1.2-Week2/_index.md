### Week 2 Objectives:

* Understand the content and practical requirements of Module 01.
* Study AWS cost optimization concepts and learn how to monitor costs through AWS Budgets.
* Understand the pricing mechanisms of core AWS services such as EC2 and S3.
* Practice basic IAM permission management and review the general cloud application deployment process.

### Week 2 Achievements:

* Completed the initial learning path of Module 01 and summarized the key AWS concepts used in the workshop.
* Understood the reason cloud cost control must be planned from the beginning of a project.
* Configured and reviewed AWS Budget settings for cost monitoring and alerting.
* Identified the main cost factors of EC2 and S3, including compute time, instance type, storage usage, and data transfer.
