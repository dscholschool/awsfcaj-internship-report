## 1. General Event Information

* **Event Name:** Saturday Meet Up
* **Organizer:** Bootcamp First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)
* **Speakers:** Huynh Hoang Long, Nguyen Tuan Thinh, Do Hoang Hieu

## 2. Rules and Competition Format

The event was organized as a knowledge tournament about the cloud ecosystem, bringing an extremely exciting and dramatic atmosphere.
* **Tournament Structure:** A total of 8 teams participated, divided into 4 direct knockout pairs. Winning teams advanced to the next round, while losing teams were eliminated.
* **Competition Method:** In each match, two teams discussed and agreed to choose 1 out of 8 pre-prepared question sets from the organizers. Each set consisted of 10 questions revolving around AWS services.
* **Scoring System:** Correct answers added points, while incorrect answers deducted the corresponding points of that question (each question carried a different point weight depending on its difficulty)
* **Lifelines:** Strategic elements reached a climax as each team had 2 lifelines per match:
  * **Risk Mitigation:** Protected the current score, with no points deducted for an incorrect answer.
  * **Star of Hope:** Doubled the score for a correct answer, but conversely, double the points were deducted for an incorrect answer (All-in).

## 3. Event Progress

Within the framework of that day's event, the tournament witnessed matches belonging to the Quarter-finals and Semi-finals. The atmosphere was intensely vibrant and tense as teams competed for every single point. The deployment of lifelines created unpredictable turning points:
* Spectacular comebacks, securing final victories despite being led early by opponents.
* Clever strategies when teams activated "Risk Mitigation" on tricky questions to preserve their existing advantage.
* Regrettable "self-destruction" moments when teams decided to play "all-in" with the "Star of Hope" but failed.

After intense rounds, the event identified the 2 best teams to enter the Finals, scheduled to face each other directly in 2 weeks.

## 4. Summary and Takeaways

Attending and following the "Saturday Meet Up" tournament was not only an entertainment activity but also brought immense professional value:
* **Knowledge Review:** Through the competition questions, I had the opportunity to systematically review all the cloud infrastructure knowledge learned in the Bootcamp video series modules.
* **Understanding Real-World Application (Use Cases):** Throughout the event, speakers shared numerous practical examples of how enterprises operate on AWS. I was particularly impressed by and remembered two typical case studies:
  * An AWS architecture system applied to a car manufacturer's platform.
  * How cloud data streams support operation for a complex weather forecasting system.

This was an extremely beneficial event, helping me solidify both foundational theory and practical perspectives before starting to implement the final project.
