### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment** The program provides an incredibly collaborative, open, and highly technical learning environment. The workspace and platforms used for tracking progress are well-structured, allowing me to fully focus on my milestones. The technical community is supportive, fostering a culture where knowledge is shared freely.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin** The mentors and administrators are exceptional. Whenever I encountered complex architectural bottlenecks — such as advanced VPC routing rules, IAM policy configurations, or RDS security group restrictions — the admin team and mentors provided precise, constructive feedback. I highly appreciate that the mentors do not simply give away the solutions; instead, they guide us through structural reasoning, encouraging us to troubleshoot and master infrastructure debugging.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major** As an Information Technology student specializing in system deployment and data architecture, the bootcamp's curriculum perfectly aligned with my academic goals. It successfully bridged the gap between traditional software development and scalable cloud systems. It allowed me to transform my existing database programming knowledge (such as writing Stored Procedures and Triggers) into high-performance cloud applications leveraging Amazon RDS, Amazon S3 Data Lakes, and secure EC2 clusters.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities** The opportunities for growth were immense. Moving through the intensive modules enabled me to advance from a local development mindset to a robust cloud engineering perspective. I mastered core concepts in FinOps (cloud cost optimization), multi-tier secure networking (VPC, subnets, NAT Gateways), and automated infrastructure provisioning (EC2 User Data). Additionally, presenting my architecture design to the administrators significantly sharpened my technical documentation and communication skills.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit** The engineering culture is highly professional yet deeply encouraging. Review sessions for our project prototypes were treated with rigorous industry standards, yet the team spirit remained motivating. Seeing everyone, from interns to senior admins, actively participate in discussions and support one another creates a strong sense of belonging and collective growth.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits** The provision of startup AWS credits ($100 credits) was an outstanding benefit, allowing me to comfortably run real-world labs, experiment with resource scaling, and practice cost-control strategies without financial constraints. Furthermore, access to exclusive meetups and technical workshops added immense value to the overall experience.

### Internship Reflection & Review

* **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?** The most satisfying aspect was witnessing our local backend prototype successfully scale into a fully operational, multi-tier cloud infrastructure on AWS. Seeing a customer payment trigger a seamless end-to-end flow — routing through the Application Load Balancer, hitting the isolated EC2 servers in a private subnet, executing automated database Triggers on Amazon RDS, and securely opening up file access via an S3 Gateway Endpoint — gave me immense confidence in my practical engineering capabilities.

* **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?** The current curriculum is highly comprehensive, but the learning curve for advanced networking and IAM policies can be quite steep in the first few weeks. It would be highly beneficial to introduce a few mini-troubleshooting workshops early in the program, specifically focusing on common infrastructure errors and log analysis, to help interns adapt faster.

* **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?** Absolutely. I would highly recommend this program to any IT student who wants to transition from pure theory to rigorous, real-world cloud deployment. The structural focus on security (Zero Trust, Least Privilege) and cost efficiency (FinOps) prepares you completely for modern enterprise demands.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

* **Suggestions to improve the internship experience:** I suggest integrating more real-time architecture review sessions earlier in the schedule. Getting technical feedback from admins directly on intermediate designs would prevent minor misconceptions from rolling over into subsequent configuration phases.
* **Desire for future collaboration:** I am highly interested in continuing my engagement with this program or corporate ecosystem, particularly in roles involving cloud data solutions, automated pipelines, or data architecture management.
* **Final Comments:** I would like to express my deepest gratitude to the entire FCJ team, mentors, and administrators. This journey has not only solidified my cloud engineering foundation but has completely transformed my approach to building secure, scalable, and cost-effective distributed systems.
